Privacy Policy — UrduLingo - Urdu Learning App

Last Updated: 11/24/2025

Thank you for using the UrduLingo App (“we”, “our”, or “the App”). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

The UrduLingo App does not collect or store any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Login or account information
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Photos, videos, or files
  • Financial or payment information

The app works fully without creating an account.

2. Information We May Collect Automatically

To improve app performance, we may collect limited non-personal data such as:

  • Device type (iPhone model, OS version)
  • Crash data
  • App usage statistics (lessons opened, time spent)

This information is:

  • Non-identifiable
  • Used only for improving the app
  • Processed via Apple-provided analytics tools

3. Advertising and Third-Party Services (AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our app. AdMob may collect and use certain data to show you relevant ads, including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID / IDFA)
  • IP address (for approximate location)
  • App usage information
  • Ad interaction data

This data is used for:

  • Serving ads
  • Measuring ad performance
  • Fraud prevention

4. Tracking Disclosure (iOS ATT)

Our app may request permission to track your activity across other companies' apps and websites through Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework.

You may deny this permission at any time. Ads will still display, but they may be less relevant.

5. Third-Party Links and Policies

For more information on how Google handles data, please review:

  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Advertising Policies: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

6. Children’s Privacy

The app is intended for users age 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

AdMob ads are configured for general audiences.

7. How We Use Data

Any non-personal data collected is used only for:

  • Improving app functionality
  • Enhancing lesson content
  • Fixing bugs or crashes
  • Understanding app performance

We do not sell or share your information with third parties.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect the information handled by the app. Since we do not collect personal data, no sensitive information is stored on our servers.

9. Your Rights

You may delete the app at any time to remove all locally stored data. We do not retain personal information.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy
